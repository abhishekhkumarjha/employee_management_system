Database Schema Basics
Copy
Employees
- employee_id (PK)
- first_name
- last_name
- email
- phone
- hire_date
- job_title
- department_id (FK)
- manager_id (FK to Employees)
- salary
- status (active/inactive)

Departments
- department_id (PK)
- department_name
- manager_id (FK to Employees)

Attendance
- attendance_id (PK)
- employee_id (FK)
- date
- check_in
- check_out
- status (present/absent/late)

LeaveRequests
- leave_id (PK)
- employee_id (FK)
- start_date
- end_date
- leave_type
- status (pending/approved/rejected)
- reason
